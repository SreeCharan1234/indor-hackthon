About Us

Saarthi, your one-stop platform for enhancing Community engagement and local connections. Our goal is to bring residents together through live feeds and news updates, helping you stay informed about local events and announcements. We offer opportunities for volunteering and ways to give back to your Community. Our local directory makes it easy to find nearby restaurants, medical facilities, and stores. Additionally, we connect job seekers with local employers to help reduce unemployment. Through our platform, you can also buy and sell goods within your Community, supporting small businesses. In case of emergencies, our platform allows you to quickly contact nearby doctors. Saarthi is dedicated to fostering a strong, interconnected Community where everyone can contribute, connect, and grow. Join us today and be a part of something great.

Frequently Asked Questions

Some frequently asked questions answers are given below,

The live feed includes a variety of events such as local Community meetings, festivals, workshops, sporting events, and more. You can find real-time updates and details about what's happening in your Community.

To sell a product, go to the "Marketplace" section and click on "Sell a Product." Provide details such as the product name, description, price, and photos. Once submitted, your listing will be visible to potential buyers in the Community.

To post a job opening, go to the "Jobs" section and click on "Post a Job." Fill in the job details, including title, description, requirements, and contact information. Your job posting will be visible to the Community once approved.

If you need assistance, you can contact our customer support team by clicking on the "Contact Us" link at the bottom of the page. Fill out the form with your query and our support team will get back to you as soon as possible
